About Oscar

Science
meets sweat.

Oscar Esquivel is a food scientist with a deep passion for physical wellbeing. His background in science shaped a core belief: that physical health is one of the most powerful drivers of mental and emotional wellness. The body and the mind are not separate — they move together.

With over 10 years in the fitness industry, Oscar recently opened his own gyms — but his mission goes further. He wants more people to access the knowledge that only years on the floor can give you: how to move well, how to avoid injury, and how to build a body that actually performs. Every program he designs is grounded in proper execution, smart progression, and long-term results.

No gimmicks. No shortcuts. Just well-designed training, built on science and refined through experience.
